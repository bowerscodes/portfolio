Who am i?


I'm James. I've loved creating things for as long as I can remember - from tinkering & crafting as a young child, to computer-building, editing, animation, web development and game-modding in adolescence. As a product of the nineties, I also grew up alongside the internet - it was ugly as hell, but I was obsessed, and still am.


After studying for my degree in Aerospace Engineering, I landed a career spanning Engineering, Project Management and Compliance. I've gained a great set of skills - but without realising, I'd taken my eye off the ball with Tech. So this portfolio documents my journey back! To find out how it's going, check out what I've been working on in the projects section - or, even better, get in touch!
