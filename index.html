Who am i?


I'm James. I've loved creating things for as long as I can remember - from the physical tinkering & crafting as a young child, to the computer-building, editing, animation, web development and game-modding which defined my adolescence. As a proud nineties child, I was privileged enough to experience growing up at the turn of the century - when the internet was also growing up - and I was obsessed.


Upon finishing school, and inspired by the hours spent playing Flight Simulator on my computer, I decided study a degree in Aerospace Engineering. This led to an Engineering career in Project Management and Compliance, which equipped me with a great set of skills - but without realising, life took over and I took my eye off the ball with Tech. So this portfolio documents my journey back! Find out how it's going, and see what I've been working on, in the projects section!
